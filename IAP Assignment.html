Barack Obama

Last Known Address: 2446 Belmont Road NW, Washington, D.C.

Biography

Education

Barack Obama attended Occidental College, Columbia University, and Harvard Law School.

Employment History

He worked as a community organizer, a civil rights attorney, a professor, and served as the 44th President of the United States.

Other Relevant Information

Obama is known for his efforts in healthcare reform and environmental protection.

Awards and Distinctions

  • Nobel Peace Prize - 2009
  • Grammy Award for Best Spoken Word Album - 2006, 2008
  • Profile in Courage Award - 2017
  • Time Person of the Year - 2008, 2012
  • NAACP Image Award - 2013

Contributions

  • Healthcare reform initiatives
  • Climate change awareness and policy advocacy
  • Economic stimulus package during the Great Recession

  • Global nuclear disarmament efforts
  • Equal Pay Act and Lily Ledbetter Fair Pay Act
  • Immigration reform
  • Renewable energy investments
  • Strengthening relations with Cuba and Iran
